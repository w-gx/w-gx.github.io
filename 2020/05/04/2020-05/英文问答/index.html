英文问答

发表于2020-05-04|更新于2020-05-04|考研复试英语
|字数总计:970|阅读时长:5分钟|阅读量:

Q1：What are the plans for the future ? / What are the plans for the postgraduate period？

A : I am looking forward to making up a solid foundation for future profession which is based on three years of study here, for this reason, I will get more experience in the participation of some relevant projects of my major.

Q2 : What is your greatest weakness?

A：Maybe because I am young people, I am a slowcoach in daily life, fortunately I have realized it was disadvantage, and I am trying my best to get rid of it.

Q3：What is your greatest strength ?

A：I think my greatest advantage is that I can persevere in getting things done， I feel a real sense of accomplishment when I finish a job and it turns out just as I'd planned.

Q4：Have you ever participated in any activities?/ What meaningful activities did you participate in during undergraduate？

A：During my college years, I participated in the Chinese Mathematics Competition. I have put a lot of time and energy into this competition, and I have finally achieved satisfactory results ,which is the national second prize.

Q5：What kind of graduate student do you want to study?/What research direction do you expect during your graduate studies?

A：Since my graduation project title is "Quantum Neural Network Model and Algorithm Based on Controlled Revolving Gate", I have a strong interest in the fields of machine learning and deep learning. At the same time, I am also interested in exploring every area that I have not yet known.

Q6：What about being eliminated ？/If you failed this time what will you do in the near future?

A：If I am not accepted, I will feel very sorry, because I have made a lot of efforts, but I will try to adjust my mentality, reflect on which aspects of my failure to do a good job, find out the problem, learn the advantages of others.No matter what result is. I never give up my pursuit and my dream.

Q7：What specialties do you have in your hometown?

A：as far as I know ,The specialties in my hometown are Harbin Red Sausage, Govas, and Wuchang Rice, which are all famous throughout the country.

Q8：Why do you want to take the postgraduate entrance examination? Why did you choose this major?

A ：with the time going on, the more I experienced, the clearer I realized that I really interested in the computer-related knowledge. Therefore, I need to go to a higher school for further study.

Q9 : What do you usually do in your spare time?

A :In my spare time, I play Chess, jog, read the news, get to know the current events, and also read some computer-related articles.

Q10 :Why did you apply to Maritime University ? /Why do you want to come to Maritime University?

A : Firstly, the school has a good learning platform for me, and the location is excellent, secondly, my senior schoolmate recommended this school because it has excellent teachers and a strong learning atmosphere.

Q11 : Please introduce your family.

A : I come from the suburb of Harbin. There are four people in my family, my parents, my sister and I. My sister lives in Harbin and my parents are farmers. Although I was born in an ordinary family, I feel very happy.

Q12 : Introduce your hometown.

A : Harbin has always been known as "Oriental Little Paris" and "Oriental Moscow", mainly because of its architectural features. At the same time, Harbin is also a hot tourist city and a famous international ice and snow cultural city.

Q13 : If you like Dalian, where have you been to Dalian?

A: Dalian is a well-developed city in the three northeastern provinces with suitable weather and good geographical location. To tell you the truth, I have not been to Dalian. But in the future, I hope I can get to know other places in Dalian.

Q14 : Introduce your university.

A: I graduated from the Northeast Petroleum University which has a long history. There are many excellent teachers in our university ,and the environment of study is very well. I appreciate my university because it give me a lot of meanningful knowledge.

Q15 : What is your favorite sport?

A: My favorite sport is running,I feel realxed when I run in the school playground, especially before or after exam. Besides, running makes me more confident to face the difficulties, and makes me have a healthy body.

Q16 : What is your favorite music?

A:I am a person who likes to listen to music in my free time. My favorite movie is "When you believe". The music has a great incentive to me. This lyrics gives me confidence to face various difficulties.

Q17 : What is your favorite book?

A: My favorite book is "Ordinary World" written by Lu Yao. The stories in the book have a great incentive to me. This book gives me confidence to face various difficulties. I also like to read some computer related books, such as "Machine Learning" written by Zhou Zhihua.

Q18 : What is your favorite movie?

A: I am a person who likes to watch movies in my free time. My favorite movie is "The Pursuit of Happyness". The story in the movie has a great incentive to me. This movie gives me confidence to face various difficulties.
